About
SUM+MIT is a tool that performs image classification on any simple math equation given to it, and then proceeds to solve it. This includes addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division.

SUM+MIT was created by the 2019 cohort of the Google Applied Machine Learning Intensive. The goal for project SUM+MIT was to put what the participants learned into practice. As such, team SUM+MIT decided to make a tool that performs image preprocessing on an equation, uses a convolutional neural network to perform classification, orders the predictions sequentially based on how the equation is written, and returns the solution to the equation.
